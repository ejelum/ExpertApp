My Story

I am an environmental scientist, UX/UI designer/ frontend developer with a vast background in life sciences and analytical chemistry. For more than a decade, I have gained extensive knowledge in research and development in the field of remediation technologies, using enzymes from biological materials. The drive to solve complex things in small simpler steps informed my transitioning into the user experience field. I want to create systems and user experiences that mimic biological processes.
