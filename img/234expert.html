Discovery Phase

Background

Current situation in the world due to the corona pandemic has opened a range of newer and larger frontiers to how business and services are being run, with greater emphasis being placed on online services. More people are now currently forced to stay and work from home. The release of an expert app, that can proffer expert solutions to different request from users would be a great way forward especially at this time.

Problem Statement

Users need a way to seek the help of an expert whenever confronted with a problem easily and quickly without much hassle of navigating through several pages before achieving this. They also want a means to save sessions with experts and also have privacy control.

Potential Solutions

The 234xpert app has a user friendly interface and well defined job/field categories that will minimize the time needed by the user to connect with desired expert. It is a platform where users can quickly and easily connect with an expert, whenever they are confronted with a problem.

Insight from Surveys and Interviews

  • It was observed that, popular areas where users seek help, in regard to household items include; electrical and plumbing.
  • Most users do not have adequate awareness of specialized expert apps besides Google and YouTube.
  • Users share one common goal and desire; i.e. a quick and reliable solution, whenever confronted with common household problem.
  • Users would like the option of speaking to a professional by video call as long as they know; these experts are verified and certified by the vendors.
  • Users desire timely response on demand, including; information on where to buy materials if need be, with clear directions on way to go.
  • Most users would prefer an expert close by.

Final Project Notes

Images (png) were optimized for web (mobile) by using image width of approximately 600 px for the images used in this project. General language of app is simple and moderate; with strategic use of colors that brings a sense of relaxation and trust to the users. Black color was used for the landing and onboarding page to create a soothing or relaxing feel. The use of primary color blue gives the user a feeling of trust. Attention was also paid to issues of accessibility; virtual representations and contrast ratios, iconographies etc
